Mission

Introduce people who have little or no experience in technology space to this exciting and highly topical hobby. Meet at least once a month, share ideas and knowledge about electronics, facilitate the construction of some devices will be useful at day to day work & life

Who are we!

IoT Geeks is a non-profit hobbyist technology community in Chennai. We at IoT Geeks constantly educating people about emerging technologies such as Internet of Things (IoT), Robotics, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality(AR), 3D-Printing, Data Analytics, Machine Learning and Autonomous.

What we do!

We connect students, professionals and entrepreneurs through our monthly workshops and meetups. This gives enormous opportunities for people stay connected on industry trends and get prepared for an opportunity ahead

How can we help!

By exposing industry trending cutting-edge technologies to students at their early ages in schools/colleges will have a profound impact on their career development. We believe, through our workshops, meetups and hands-on trainings we will able to constantly mentor students and with their active participation - make them next gen innovators, makers, technology leaders and young entrepreneurs
